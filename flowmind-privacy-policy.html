Section 01

Who We Are

FlowMind is a personal productivity automation system operated by its individual user. It is not a commercial SaaS product or a company. The system is built on third-party platforms including n8n Cloud, Anthropic Claude API, Meta WhatsApp Business API, and Google Workspace APIs. Each of these platforms has its own privacy policy, which applies independently.

This Privacy Policy describes how data flows through the FlowMind system, what is retained, where it is stored, and what rights you have over your information.

Scope of this policy

This policy applies to data processed by the FlowMind automation system when you send WhatsApp messages to the associated business number. It does not govern the platforms FlowMind is built upon — each has its own policy linked in Section 4.

Section 02

What Data We Collect

FlowMind collects and processes the following categories of data:

Data type Description Storage
WhatsApp message content Text of messages sent to the FlowMind business number Processed in real time — not stored by FlowMind
Sender phone number Your WhatsApp phone number Used to send confirmation replies only
Sender name Your WhatsApp display name Logged in your Google Sheet
Message timestamp Date and time of each message Logged in your Google Sheet
AI classification output Task title, due date, priority, classification type Stored in your Google Tasks / Calendar / Sheet
Google account data Calendar events, task lists, Gmail drafts, Drive files Your own Google account only

FlowMind does NOT collect:

  • Passwords or authentication credentials (handled entirely by OAuth 2.0)
  • Payment information of any kind
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or browser fingerprints
  • Data from contacts other than the sender of a message
  • Voice messages, images, or media files (text messages only)
  • Biometric data of any kind
Section 03

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following personal productivity purposes:

Purpose How your data is used
Task creation Message content is classified by AI and converted into Google Tasks entries
Calendar scheduling Detected events and deadlines are added to your Google Calendar
Email drafting Action-required messages trigger a draft reply in your Gmail
Activity logging Each processed message is logged to your private Google Sheet for your own review
Confirmation replies Your phone number is used to send a WhatsApp confirmation back to you
Weekly review Scheduled prompts sent to your number on Sunday evenings
Morning briefing A daily summary of top tasks sent on weekday mornings
No commercial use

Your data is never used for advertising, profiling, machine learning model training, or any purpose beyond your personal productivity workflow. Data is never sold or shared with third parties for commercial gain.

Service Role Data shared Privacy Policy

Message content is sent to the Claude API for classification only. Anthropic does not store message content from API calls for model training purposes. API data is subject to Anthropic's API usage policy. See anthropic.com/privacy for full details.

Section 05

Data Storage & Retention

All personal data processed by FlowMind is stored exclusively in your own Google account. FlowMind does not operate its own database or storage server.

Data Where stored Retention
Google Tasks Your Google account Until you delete the task
Google Calendar events Your Google account Until you delete the event
Gmail drafts Your Google account Until you delete or send the draft
Google Sheets log Your Google account Until you delete the sheet or row
n8n execution logs n8n Cloud (metadata only) Automatically deleted after 30 days
WhatsApp messages Meta infrastructure Per Meta's data retention policy

n8n Cloud stores workflow execution metadata (node names, timestamps, success/error status) for debugging purposes. Message content is explicitly excluded from n8n execution logs by the workflow configuration.

Section 06

Security Measures

Measure Description
OAuth 2.0 All Google API access uses OAuth 2.0 — no passwords are stored anywhere in the system
HMAC-SHA256 verification Every incoming WhatsApp webhook is verified against Meta's signature before processing
HTTPS / TLS 1.3 All data in transit is encrypted across all platforms
Minimum OAuth scopes Only specific Google permissions required are requested (tasks, calendar.events, gmail.compose, drive.file)
No PII in logs Message content is excluded from n8n execution logs — only classification metadata is retained
Credential vault All API keys are stored in n8n's encrypted credential store — never in source code
Rate limiting Workflow processes a maximum of 10 messages per minute to prevent abuse
Section 07

Your Rights & Controls

As the operator and sole user of FlowMind, you have full control over your data. The following actions are available at any time:

Right How to exercise it
Access your data View all logged data in your Google Sheet and Google Workspace apps at any time
Delete your data Delete any row in the Google Sheet, any task, event, or draft from your Google account
Stop processing Deactivate the n8n workflow at any time — no further messages will be processed
Revoke access Revoke FlowMind's Google OAuth access via myaccount.google.com/permissions
Delete everything Delete the Google Sheet, revoke OAuth, and deactivate the workflow to remove all traces
Export your data Download your Google Sheet as CSV at any time for a full activity log export
Section 08

WhatsApp-Specific Terms

FlowMind uses the Meta WhatsApp Business Cloud API. By sending messages to the FlowMind business number, you acknowledge the following:

  • Messages are processed automatically by an AI system — responses are generated by Claude AI, not a human.
  • Only text messages are processed. Images, voice notes, and other media are ignored.
  • In Development mode, only pre-verified phone numbers can interact with the system.
  • Meta's WhatsApp Business Policy applies to all messages sent through the platform.
  • You may stop using the system at any time by simply not sending further messages.
  • FlowMind does not send unsolicited messages. All outbound messages are direct responses or scheduled personal productivity prompts that you have opted into.
WhatsApp Business Policy Compliance
  • FlowMind operates as a personal productivity tool for the account owner only.
  • It does not send unsolicited messages (spam).
  • All outbound messages are direct responses or agreed-upon scheduled prompts.
  • Full Meta WhatsApp Business Policy: business.whatsapp.com/policy
Section 09

Children's Privacy

FlowMind is designed for personal professional productivity use by adults. It is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. No data from minors is knowingly collected or processed. If you believe data from a minor has been collected, please contact the system operator immediately to request deletion.
